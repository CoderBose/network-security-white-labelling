How to pair with the APP?
1. Download App and installation.
2. Open the App and create your account.
3. Add the device from the upper right conner Tap"+" and select "our switch".
4. Set your WIFI connection follow the APP instructed.
5. Press any one of the ON/OFF buttons for 5 seconds and wait for the LED indicator flashes fast, the reset is successful. EZ mode is available and wait for connected.
6. Or when fast flashing, then press any one of the ON/OFF buttons for 5s,the LED indicator become blinking slow. AP mode is available and choose
"SmartLife-XXX" hot spot, then connected. Wait for the connection till successful.
Please Note: : Only support 2.4Ghz WiFi while connecting with APP; When the socket changed network environment, you need to restore the factory settings, and then reconnect to the network.
Once connected, you can use your phone via 4G or 5G WiFi to turn on/off the smart switch anywhere, Please keep the smart switch stay on 2.4G WiFi range always.
